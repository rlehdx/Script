# ScriptFlow AI — Growth Tactics (Execute After Purchase)

10 high-leverage growth moves you can execute immediately after buying ScriptFlow.

---

## 1. Product Hunt Launch

**Effort:** Medium | **Timeline:** 1–2 weeks | **Potential:** 500–2,000 signups in 24 hours

- Create a "coming soon" page on Product Hunt and collect upvotes
- Prepare a 60-second demo video showing a script generating live
- Schedule your launch for Tuesday–Thursday, 12:01 AM PST
- Prep 50+ supporters to upvote and comment on launch day
- Write your tagline: "Turn any idea into a high-converting script in 10 seconds"

---

## 2. Twitter/X Creator Community

**Effort:** Low | **Timeline:** Ongoing | **Potential:** 50–200 signups/week

- Post a "I scripted 30 YouTube videos in 2 hours" thread showing real output
- Tag creators in your niche with "@[creator] this would've saved you 3 hours"
- Create a "Script of the Day" — share a sample ScriptFlow output daily
- Reply to creators complaining about writer's block with a ScriptFlow demo

---

## 3. YouTube Niche Communities

**Effort:** Low | **Timeline:** Week 1 | **Potential:** 100–500 signups

- Post in YouTube creator subreddits (r/youtubers, r/NewTubers): "I built a tool that scripts my videos — here's the output"
- Share in Facebook Groups for YouTubers, TikTok creators, and copywriters
- Post in Skool communities for content creators

---

## 4. AppSumo or LifetimeDeals.com

**Effort:** Medium | **Timeline:** 2–4 weeks | **Potential:** $5,000–$50,000 lump sum + 200–2,000 users

- Apply to AppSumo marketplace with a lifetime deal offer (e.g. $99 for lifetime Pro access, limited to 500 codes)
- This gives you upfront cash + a large user base that becomes your best word-of-mouth engine
- Also list on: PitchGround, DealMirror, SaasMantra

---

## 5. Affiliate Program

**Effort:** Low | **Timeline:** 1 week | **Potential:** 20–40% organic growth from affiliates

- Use Rewardful or FirstPromoter (integrates with Stripe in 30 minutes)
- Offer 30% commission recurring for 12 months
- Reach out to 10–20 YouTube creators in the productivity/creator economy niche
- "Make $57/year per referral — just post one video"

---

## 6. Cold Outreach to Freelance Copywriters

**Effort:** Medium | **Timeline:** 2–3 weeks | **Potential:** 50–200 Pro signups

- Find copywriters on Fiverr, Upwork, LinkedIn who sell VSL scripts or ad copy
- Email: "Hey [Name], I built a tool that generates a first-draft VSL in 30 seconds — try it free, no card required"
- Offer a special discount code for early freelancers who sign up and refer clients

---

## 7. SEO Content

**Effort:** Medium | **Timeline:** 3–6 months | **Potential:** Compounding organic traffic

Write 10 blog posts targeting high-intent keywords:
- "YouTube script generator free"
- "AI VSL script generator"
- "TikTok hook generator AI"
- "Facebook ad script AI"
- "how to write a YouTube video script"

Use your own ScriptFlow output as examples in every post.

## 8. Niche Script Packs (One-Time Product)

**Effort:** Low | **Timeline:** 1 week | **Potential:** $2,000–$10,000 additional revenue**

Create pre-made "Script Packs" as a one-time Gumroad product:
- "30 YouTube Hook Scripts for Finance Creators" — $29
- "20 High-Converting TikTok Scripts for E-commerce" — $29
- "15 VSL Scripts for Online Course Creators" — $49

Each pack links back to ScriptFlow for customization, driving free-to-Pro conversion.

## 9. Testimonial + Case Study Machine

**Effort:** Low | **Timeline:** Ongoing | **Potential:** Significant trust-building for conversions**

- Email every new Pro subscriber after 7 days: "Got any early wins with ScriptFlow?"
- Collect video testimonials using Testimonial.to (free tier available)
- Add real video testimonials to the landing page — they convert 3–5x better than text
- Run a "share your script results" campaign with a small prize ($50 gift card)

## 10. Agency Upsell Tier

**Effort:** Medium | **Timeline:** 1–2 months | **Potential:** $500–$2,000 additional MRR with minimal users

Add an **Agency plan** at $99/month:
- 5 team member seats
- 1,000 scripts/month
- White-label-ready outputs
- Dedicated onboarding email

Target: digital marketing agencies, video production companies, freelance copywriters with clients.

Implementation: Add team seats to Supabase user schema, update middleware to check seat count. Estimated dev time: 4–8 hours.
